About FastMathFacts.io

An intentional training platform designed to reward both tactical fluency and gritty determination with instantaneous feedback.

Our Core Philosophy

Traditional math flashcards often measure one thing: how fast a student can recall a number. They don't measure how hard a student worked to get that mastery.

FastMathFacts flips the script by mathematically isolating natural processing speed from hard work. Our engine utilizes two distinct feedback loops to ensure every student feels the reward of their own cognitive growth:

🏆 Mastery Score

Measures accuracy and processing efficiency. As student's answer faster, they get bigger point rewards. Once a threshold is shattered, it is permanently locked in.

⚡ Determination Score

Measures grit and cognitive effort. Students receive zero points for repeating concepts they can already solve effortlessly. Instead, huge rewards are granted for targeting their weak spots, working through friction points, and breaking personal records.

Privacy by Default (GDPR Compliant)

We believe that a student's learning workspace should be completely private. FastMathFacts is architected from the ground up to respect strict international data standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Local Isolation: By default, all training records, rolling speed profiles, and system levels remain stored directly within your web browser. No personal data ever leaves your hardware device.
  • Anonymized Public Ranks: Opting into our international leaderboard is entirely optional. Rankings are populated strictly by a customized username or set of initials to maintain total identity protection.
  • Efficacy Aggregation: We internally track anonymized learning trends (such as average accuracy rates and mastery gains across generalized age brackets) exclusively to evaluate the educational quality of our platform and refine it over time.
